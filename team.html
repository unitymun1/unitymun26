Meet the Leadership Team

At UNITY MUN, our leadership team is dedicated to creating a professional, engaging, and impactful Model United Nations experience. With a shared commitment to diplomacy, teamwork, and excellence, the Secretariat works tirelessly to ensure the success of the conference.

Secretary-General

Celina Zakaria

The Secretary-General oversees all aspects of the conference, providing vision, leadership, and strategic direction to ensure UNITY MUN upholds the highest standards of academic and organizational excellence.

Deputy Secretary-General

Saif Maghairah

The Deputy Secretary-General supports the overall management of the conference, coordinating with committees and teams to ensure smooth operations and an exceptional delegate experience.
