What Is UNITY MUN?

UNITY MUN (Model United Nations) is an educational simulation that brings together students and youth to engage in diplomacy, debate, and international relations. Participants represent countries, organizations, and institutions as they discuss real global issues, negotiate solutions, and work collaboratively to draft resolutions.

UNITY MUN aims to empower young leaders by enhancing critical thinking, public speaking, leadership, and negotiation skills in a professional and inclusive environment that reflects the values of unity, dialogue, and global cooperation.

Theme: Voices of the Generation

Voices of the Generation represents the power of youth in shaping the present and the future. This theme highlights the importance of giving young people a platform to express their ideas, concerns, and solutions to global challenges.

Through this theme, UNITY MUN encourages delegates to speak up, take responsibility, and use their voices to promote change, innovation, and unity in an ever-evolving world.
