Committees

General Assembly (GA)

The General Assembly is the main deliberative body of the United Nations, bringing together all member states to discuss global challenges, promote international cooperation, and develop solutions through diplomacy and dialogue.

  • The Impact of Modern Technology on the International Community
  • Regulation of Autonomous Weapons Systems

Human Rights Council (HRC)

The Human Rights Council is responsible for promoting and protecting human rights around the world, addressing violations and ensuring dignity and equality for all.

  • Accountability Toward Syrian Refugees
  • Artificial Intelligence and Workers’ Rights

Security Council (SC)

The Security Council is tasked with maintaining international peace and security, addressing conflicts, sanctions, and global threats.

  • NATO–Russia Rising Tensions
  • UNSC Sanctions and Their Global Impacts

International Criminal Court (ICC)

The ICC seeks justice for victims of genocide, war crimes, and crimes against humanity.

  • The Case of Saif al-Islam Gaddafi
  • Child Soldier Recruitment and Justice

Jordanian House of Representatives

This committee simulates the Jordanian legislative body, focusing on national policymaking and legal reform.

  • قانون الجرائم الإلكترونية
  • الطاقة المتجددة وتقليل كلفة الطاقة

INTERPOL

INTERPOL facilitates international police cooperation to combat transnational crime and enhance global security.

  • Crime Prevention and Criminal Justice
  • Cybercrime
