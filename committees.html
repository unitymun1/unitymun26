Committees

General Assembly (GA)

Introduction
The General Assembly is the main deliberative body of the United Nations, bringing together all member states to discuss global challenges, promote international cooperation, and develop solutions through diplomacy and dialogue.

Topics

  • The Impact of Modern Technology on the International Community
    Examining how technological advancements affect global security, economic development, communication, and international relations.
  • Regulation of Autonomous Weapons Systems
    Addressing the ethical, legal, and security implications of autonomous weapons and the need for international regulation.

Human Rights Council (HRC)

Introduction
The Human Rights Council is responsible for promoting and protecting human rights around the world.

Topics

  • Accountability and Responsibility Toward Syrian Refugees Under International Human Rights Frameworks
    Assessing international obligations and shared responsibility for the protection and rights of Syrian refugees.
  • Artificial Intelligence and the Protection of Workers’ Rights
    Exploring the impact of AI on employment, labor rights, and the future of work.

Security Council (SC)

Introduction
The Security Council is tasked with maintaining international peace and security.

Topics

  • NATO–Russia Rising Tensions in the Black Sea
    Analyzing escalating geopolitical tensions and their implications for regional and global security.
  • UNSC Sanctions and Their Global Impacts on Security and Effectiveness
    Evaluating the effectiveness of sanctions and their consequences on international stability and humanitarian conditions.

International Criminal Court (ICC)

Introduction
The International Criminal Court seeks justice for victims of genocide, crimes against humanity, and war crimes.

Topics

  • Ensuring Accountability for Crimes Against Humanity: The Case of Saif al-Islam Gaddafi
  • Child Soldier Recruitment and International Justice: Lessons from Thomas Lubanga Dyilo
