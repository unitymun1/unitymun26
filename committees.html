Committees

General Assembly (GA)

The General Assembly serves as the main deliberative body of the United Nations, where all member states have equal representation. Delegates debate global issues affecting international peace, development, and cooperation.

  • The Impact of Modern Technology on Global Stability
  • Regulation of Autonomous Weapons Systems

Human Rights Council (HRC)

The Human Rights Council focuses on the promotion and protection of human rights around the world, addressing violations and proposing international solutions.

  • Accountability Toward Syrian Refugees
  • The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Workers’ Rights

Security Council (SC)

The Security Council is responsible for maintaining international peace and security, addressing conflicts, sanctions, and threats to global stability.

  • NATO–Russia Tensions
  • Sanctions and Their Impact on Global Security

International Criminal Court (ICC)

The ICC investigates and prosecutes individuals accused of genocide, war crimes, crimes against humanity, and aggression.

  • The Case of Saif al-Islam Gaddafi
  • Child Soldier Recruitment and Accountability

Jordanian House of Representatives

This committee simulates the Jordanian legislative body, focusing on national laws and policies relevant to Jordan’s future.

  • قانون الجرائم الإلكترونية
  • سياسات الطاقة المتجددة في الأردن

INTERPOL

INTERPOL facilitates international police cooperation to combat transnational crime and ensure global security.

  • Combating Cybercrime
  • International Crime Prevention Strategies
