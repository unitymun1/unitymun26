Committees

UNITY MUN features carefully designed committees that allow delegates to engage in meaningful debate, diplomacy, and collaborative problem-solving.

Each committee is guided by experienced chairs and focuses on real global issues that challenge today’s world.

Delegates are expected to research thoroughly, represent their assigned country or position with accuracy, and participate actively in moderated and unmoderated debates.

Through these committees, UNITY MUN aims to provide an enriching academic experience that develops leadership, confidence, and international awareness.
